About Loaddr

What it is

Loaddr allows you to create fixed bitcoin addresses, called “loaders”, which you can configure to do almost anything.

Configure your LOADRR to
  • Top up your phone
  • Convert bitcoins into fiat
  • Split your funds according to a budget
  • More coming soon!
Each loader will carefully report to you what it’s doing in real time
  • [8:01]:Received 132,000 bits…
  • [8:01]:Depositing to Coinbase…
  • [8:03]:Selling 132,000 bits for 115 USD…
  • [8:03]:Sell completed!

Every time a loader receives bits, it spends them in the way you configured it.
Think of them as shortcuts, which you can save and use as many times as you want.
Simply add them as contacts to your wallet of choice.

Example

Creating a phone loader

1

Click in new “phone loader”

2

Choose your country and phone number

3

Save the given address to your contacts

That’s it.
Now when you want to top up your phone, just send bits to that address. You’ll receive a confirmation shortly after with the transaction details.
